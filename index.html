View how NRT works Get NRT for your country

The National Reporting Toolkit is an online platform that speeds up indicator preparation, analysis and reporting. In doing so, accurate information reaches decision-makers faster, facilitating swift and effective action to reduce environmental degradation.

Transform data into actionable indicators

The National Reporting Toolkit turns your environmental datasets into easy to understand information, which helps your team make better decisions. The dashboard provides an overview of your whole environment, or allows you to focus on thematic areas.

Build your State of Environment Reports online

The National Reporting Toolkit allows you to report on your newest available data. Using NRT, your team can edit and produce reports collaboratively from anywhere.

Understand your data with custom visualisations

Custom visualisations offer deeper insight into the trends in your data: Filter it, display it as a graph, table or map and include then in your report.

Seamlessly integrate multiple data sources

You can easily import your country’s environmental data into NRT, which then can be combined with our growing repository of international datasets.

Share and export your outputs and data

You can share your team’s reports online. NRT allows you to download the data powering your visualisations for further analysis, and also provides an API so you can use the data with your own software.

Create new versions of your reports as new data arrives

NRT notifies you as new data arrives, so you can ensure your narratives are always up-to-date. You can create new versions of your reports, updating narrative and visualisations for the new data and re-using the relevant content from previous versions.
